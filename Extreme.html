Best Extreme destinations.

If you're the type of person who will dive head first into danger just for
fun, then here are the top five most dangerous places to go.

1. Ice swimming in Finland


Previous Next

If you're not too fond of heat and looking for an activity to get your adreniline running, then why dont you try ice swimming in Finland. During the winter months, many events and festivals every year are held in both Finland and Estonia, where people have to either dip, or swim in near-frozen water. By the time these events happen the water is usually below 5 degrees. BRRRRR!!!!! if you want to give it a shot, just sign up to one of the events, but make sure you have enough blankets for after.



2. Bunjee Jumping in Colorado


Previous Next

If you're the type of person who will jump at the chance to do something life-threatening, then you should definately check out the Royal Gorge Bride. This might not seem scary but try jumping off a 1000ft bridge into a canyon and above a river, only tied on by some bunjee gear. Scary right?
