2. Wet'n'Wild in Gold Coast

Head straight to the Extreme H20 Zone if you're looking for a challenge. The exciting Blackhole takes you down a spiral into a pitch black tube slide. Or even the Near-verticle slide to get a zero gravity feel. Or if that isnt really your style you can choose the more gentler slides or you can chillax in the pools of the tropical gardens
